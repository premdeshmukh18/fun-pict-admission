Section 1

Grate:- plateform where combustion takes place Down take header function is to collect water from boiler drum Baffle:- rectangular plates direct flue gases in terms to maximize the contact of flue gases and steel tubes.(zig-zag motion) Mud hole :- to remove impurities in water stored Mountings are the essential part and parcel without the boiler can’t work Acceceries are used to improve the function of boiler. Pressure gauge is mounting which fitted to check pressure of stored steam. Water level indicator- mounting which used to check level of water inside the boiler drum. Feed level valve- non returning valve which supplies water in boiler drum. Main stop valve regulates the steam before entering the turbine part. Fusible Plug- made up of gun metal which melts on high temperature and Air pre heater acceceries Heater exanchanger used to supply pre heat to water is called economizer(acceceries) Super heater increases the temperature of already generated steam. It is Made of copper
